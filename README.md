## User Guide

### For Residents (Normal Account)

1. **Register**: Click "Register" and choose "Normal Account"
2. **Browse**: Explore businesses by category
3. **Search**: Find specific businesses or products
4. **View Details**: See full business info, products, and contact details

### For Business Owners (Business Account)

1. **Register**: Click "Register" and choose "Business Account"
2. **Create Listing**: Fill in business details, upload images
3. **Manage Products**: Add products/services with images and pricing
4. **Update Info**: Edit business details anytime from dashboard
5. **Track Status**: See if your business is approved/pending


## Features Overview

### Authentication
- Email/password registration and login
- Separate flows for business and normal accounts
- Session management

### Business Management
- Create comprehensive business profiles
- Upload multiple images (max 5)
- Contact information (phone, email, address, website)
- Category and subcategory assignment
- Statustracking (pending/approved/rejected)

### Product Catalog
- Add unlimited products per business
- Product images (max 3 per product)
- Pricing information
- Product descriptions
- Active/inactive toggle


### Search & Browse
- Category-based browsing
- Full-text search across businesses and products
- Filter by category or status
- Responsive grid layouts

## Technologies Used
